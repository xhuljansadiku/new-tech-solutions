Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024

Introduction

At MX Tech Solutions, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our website mx-techsolutions.co.uk or our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other information you provide through our contact forms, newsletter sign-ups, or service requests.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage data (collected via cookies, analytics, etc.).
  • Other Information: Any information you choose to provide in communications with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To respond to inquiries and communicate with you.
  • To process orders, service requests, or newsletter subscriptions.
  • For marketing and promotional purposes (only with your consent).
  • To ensure website security and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and analyze usage. By using our site, you agree to our use of cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

We use third-party services like Google Analytics to gather insights on website traffic and usage.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We may share information with trusted partners (such as web hosting, email marketing services, or analytics providers) only as necessary to operate our business.
  • We may disclose your information if required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data.
  • You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • To exercise your rights or for any privacy-related requests, contact us at contact.mxtech@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please email us at contact.mxtech@gmail.com.
